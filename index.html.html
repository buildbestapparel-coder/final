Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity?
MOQ depends on product type, fabric and customization method. Share your requirements with us on WhatsApp or the contact form and we will give you an accurate answer for your specific order.
How long does production take?
Standard turnaround is 2 weeks after order confirmation and sample approval. Production time may vary depending on order quantity, customization method and shipping destination.
Do you offer free samples?
Yes — confirm a bulk order of at least 30 pieces with us and we will produce a free sample so you can approve quality before full production begins.
Do you ship worldwide?
Yes. We ship worldwide through UPS, DHL, FedEx and Aramex. We handle all packaging, documentation and export coordination for international orders.
What file formats do you accept for designs?
AI, PDF, PNG, PSD, SVG and high quality reference images are preferred. If you only have a rough idea or mood board, our design team can help build a techpack from scratch.
Do you do private label and OEM production?
Yes — we specialize in OEM and private label manufacturing. We can add your custom tags, labels, hangtags and packaging so every piece ships under your brand identity.
